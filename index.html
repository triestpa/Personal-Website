Glicious

G-licious is a quick and easy way to check the Dining Menu for the Grinnell College Marketplace. You can check the menu for today, tomorrow, or any day in the next week as and when a new menu becomes available. You can also filter out choices by personal dietary concerns.

KDIC Radio

The offical app for KDIC, the Grinnell College radio station.

Grinnell Events

An always up-to-date mobile schedule of Grinnell College events. The app will provide useful information to the Grinnell Community about upcoming talks, sports games, fun events, and more! Events can be filtered by catagory, and added to the user's prefered personal calendar.

DB

DB is the mobile version of Grinnell College's campus directory. DB is available on iOS and Android. It is optimized for both phone and tablet layouts. Full data permissions are restricted for users who are not connected to campus wifi for privacy reasons.
